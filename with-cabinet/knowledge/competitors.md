## Active Competitors

### Linear
- **Pricing:** Free (250 issues), $8/user/mo (Pro), $16/user/mo (Enterprise)
- **Positioning:** Speed-first, developer-native, opinionated workflow
- **Recent moves:** Launched "Linear Asks" (async Q&A feature) in March 2026
- **Weaknesses:** No async documentation, rigid for non-eng roles, no AI agents
- **Threat level:** High (strong brand, growing fast)
- **Our angle:** We beat them when teams grow past 5 people or hire their first non-engineer

### Jira (Atlassian)
- **Pricing:** Free (10 users), $8.15/user/mo, enterprise custom
- **Positioning:** Enterprise-grade, Atlassian ecosystem lock-in
- **Recent moves:** No major product changes Q1 2026 — focused on AI Assist
- **Weaknesses:** Overwhelming complexity, slow, hated by developers
- **Threat level:** Medium (teams don't choose Jira — companies force it on them)
- **Our angle:** Migration story — "Escape Jira" positioning works well

### Height
- **Pricing:** Free, $8.50/user/mo
- **Positioning:** Flexible views, small team-friendly, modern UI
- **Recent moves:** Soft-launched an AI assistant in beta (still basic)
- **Weaknesses:** Small ecosystem, no scheduling/automation, weak AI
- **Threat level:** Medium-Low (similar ICP, less momentum)
- **Our angle:** We have better AI and a clearer roadmap
