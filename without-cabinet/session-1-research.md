## Claude's Output:

### Competitor Analysis: Project Management for Small Dev Teams

**1. Linear**
- Pricing: Free up to 250 issues, then $8/user/mo
- Positioning: Speed-first, developer-native workflow
- Weakness: Opinionated — hard to customize for non-eng teams
- Opportunity: Linear ignores async/documentation workflows entirely

**2. Jira (Atlassian)**
- Pricing: Free up to 10 users, then $8.15/user/mo
- Positioning: Enterprise-grade, deeply integrated with Atlassian suite
- Weakness: Overwhelming for small teams — steep learning curve
- Opportunity: Teams hate Jira but use it because their company forces them to

**3. Height**
- Pricing: Free, then $8.50/user/mo
- Positioning: Flexible views (list, board, calendar, gantt) for small teams
- Weakness: Less name recognition, smaller ecosystem
- Opportunity: Great UI but weak on AI features — wide open
