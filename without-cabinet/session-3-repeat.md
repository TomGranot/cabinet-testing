### DevFlow Weekly — What the Big Tools Are Missing

Hey,

We spend a lot of time looking at what other tools are doing. Linear is fast. Jira is powerful. Height is flexible. They're all good products.

But there's a pattern we keep seeing: the bigger a tool gets, the less it fits small teams.

Linear optimizes for speed — great if you're a pure eng team, not so great once you hire a designer or PM who can't parse a sprint board. Jira is built for companies with dedicated project managers and six-week planning cycles. Height is the most flexible of the bunch, but it's thin on AI features and still feels like a work in progress.

Small dev teams don't need an enterprise tool scaled down. They need something built for them from the start.

That's what we're building with DevFlow.

More soon.
